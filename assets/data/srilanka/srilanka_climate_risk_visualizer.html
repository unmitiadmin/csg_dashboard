Climate Risk Visualiser

The climate risk visualizer module provides hazards, exposure, vulnerability and lack of coping capacity to assess the risk index at the national level. The information supports policymakers and managers to prioritize climate adaptation and mitigation strategies for targeted investment to reduce the vulnerability among communities and strengthen resilient infrastructure.
