The climate risk profile component provides users with a clear understanding of current climate-related risks within a specific geographic area. It synthesizes data from various sources and models to offer a clear snapshot of historical and projected climate patterns of climate risks, such as rising temperatures and changing precipitation patterns. It supports policymakers, managers and communities with essential information to better understand and address immediate climate challenges.
