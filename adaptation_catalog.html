Development Indicator

This module showcases the climate adaptation projects from around the world that are implemented from national to local scale. Anyone who has signed up to the system can upload information about projects they are working on, with this information then becoming visible to all other users of the system. In time, the dashboard may be made available more widely, so that members of smaller enterprises and the public can also upload information on initiatives. Projects can be viewed on a map, to show where clusters of similar initiatives or gaps exist.

The Climate-Smart Governance Dashboard provides accurate data on climate-related hazards, climate scenarios, project trackers, and M&E tools to support medium to long-term adaptation planning aligned with global development goals. Specifically, it can help nations undertake the UNFCCC NAP process and integrate adaptation into new and existing national, sectoral, and local policies.
