Geo-Intelligence

The Geo-Intelligence components support users to visualize, analyze, and interpret a wide range of information for rapid assessment. The interface supports stakeholders to quickly analyze areas of high risk from climate hazards and identify those that need immediate priority. The feature also supports the selection of adaptation projects using topography, land use, and infrastructure. Geo-Intelligence facilitates evidence-based decision-making, informed project planning, and efficient resource allocation by providing a spatial perspective on climate change challenges and opportunities. It empowers stakeholders to develop climate-resilient strategies that are tailored to the specific geographic context, fostering more targeted and impactful adaptation efforts.
