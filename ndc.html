Nationally Determined Contributions Tracker

The NDC Tracking Tool allows users to assess progress toward a country's Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs) under the Paris Agreement. It provides clear data on NDC implementation by comparing planned versus actual actions and estimates greenhouse gas reductions achieved by the country. The tool supports transparency, identifies priority areas for action, and empowers countries to track progress and enhance their climate commitments, contributing to the goals of the Paris Agreement.

To align to 1.5°C compatible pathways, Sri Lanka needs to reduce its GHG emissions to 17-26 MtCO₂e/yr by 2030, or reductions of around 28-52% below 2015 levels by 2030, excluding LULUCF.
