Investment Portfolio Planning

This module provides an overview of a country, detailing the mainstays of its economy and the risks presented by climate change.

It allows users to evaluate the potential impacts on GDP under different climate scenarios in agriculture, services and industry sectors and its stress levels. Policymakers can use the module as the basis for analysis to assess the potential financial impacts of climate change within their country and use this evidence to attract investment from donors.
