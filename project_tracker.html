Adaptation

It implies the ability to learn from experiences, make necessary adjustments, and respond effectively to dynamic and evolving circumstances in order to achieve sustainable development and address pressing global issues.

Mitigation

It involves proactive measures to address environmental challenges and reduce the negative consequences of human activities. It is essential to sustainable development and efforts to create a more resilient and environmentally friendly future.

Cross-Cutting

It signifies an approach or concept that transcends boundaries and integrates multiple dimensions or elements. It emphasizes the interconnected nature of various disciplines, sectors, or issues, and encourages holistic and comprehensive approaches to problem-solving and decision-making.
