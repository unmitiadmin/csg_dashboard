Future climate Risk

Future Climate Risk is an indispensable resource for anticipating and planning the impacts of climate change. By utilizing the recent climate model and its scenario analysis, it offers a glimpse into how climate-related risks are likely to evolve in the coming years and decades. Decision-makers can use this information to assess potential future challenges, such as floods, droughts or heatwaves, and formulate strategies for adaptation and resilience to ensure long-term sustainability.
